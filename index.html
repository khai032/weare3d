Statistics about Underage Drinking

There are 2.6 million deaths annually related to alcohol consumption.

In North America, 600,000 youth (ages 12-17) have drank alcohol in their lifetime, which is 22% of their population.

1/3 of the Filipino adult population engages in heavy drinking (consuming 6 or more bottles of alcoholic beverages).

Common Effects of Drinking

Drinking alcohol can cause immediate effects such as IMPAIRED JUDGMENT and COORDINATION, increasing the risk of ACCIDENTS and injuries.

Many people experience NAUSEA and VOMITING, especially after consuming large amounts.

Alcohol also causes dehydration, which contributes to HANGOVERS, leading to headaches, fatigue, and dizziness the next day.
